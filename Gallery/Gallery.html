Our Commitment to Quality & Safety

At Baramati Foods and Beverages, our commitment to crafting delicious, trustworthy products begins in our state-of-the-art FSSAI-compliant manufacturing facility. From our fruity Mayurs Gels jellies to our wholesome Nutribels chikkis, every product is prepared under strict hygiene protocols, with a focus on cleanliness, food safety, and responsible production.

How We Make It Happen:

  • Modern, stainless-steel equipment for cooking and mixing
  • Temperature-controlled zones to ensure ingredient stability
  • Cleanroom-grade packaging areas to avoid contamination
  • Strict hygiene protocols for staff, including protective gear and regular sanitization
  • Routine quality checks at each production stage

Our Commitment to Safe & Quality Foods:

  • All products are 100% vegetarian and FSSAI certified
  • No artificial colors or harmful preservatives in our Chikkis
  • Jellies are packed using automated, touch-free machines
  • Batch testing ensures every lot meets our high standards
  • We maintain complete traceability from ingredient to package
