Spreading Sweetness Tradition & Joy in Every Bite !

Welcome to Baramati Foods and Beverages, a family-owned business crafting sweet memories since 1996. Known for our beloved Mayurs Gels, including the iconic Imali Chulbuli and a variety of fruity jellies, we bring joy to families nationwide. Expanding our legacy, we proudly introduce Nutribels, a sub-brand dedicated to healthy, wholesome snacks rooted in Indian tradition. From nostalgic flavors to nutritious treats, our products are crafted with love, trust, and innovation. With every bite, we spread sweetness, nutrition, and happiness — making moments tastier and smiles brighter for everyone!
